About

Hi there, I'm Ethan Steele! I'm a Software Engineer at Barclays, working as an Apprentice under Multiverse.

Ever since I was 6 years old, I have had a deep interest in computers. I have been fascinated by the limitless possibilities of technology and how it has continued to shape and influence our lives. My early exposure to computers set the foundation for my future career path.

To further pursue my passion, I enrolled in a dedicated course on Computer Science called T-Levels. This intensive program provided me with a comprehensive understanding of the industry and equipped me with the technical and theoretical knowledge to succeed in the field. I am grateful for the opportunity to expand my knowledge and skills.

I have had the privilege of working for two prominent game studios as a Marketing Director and General Manager. In these roles, I was responsible for overseeing marketing strategies and campaigns, as well as managing the day-to-day operations of the studio. I am proud of the success I have driven in these positions, and I believe it is a testament to my skills and expertise in the field.

I am a person who is passionate about continuous self-improvement and growth. My enthusiasm and drive to always do better have led me to where I am today. I believe in the power of hard work and determination, and I am always striving to learn new things and expand my horizons.

In my free time, I have a variety of interests and hobbies. I enjoy exploring new technologies, reading about advancements in the field, and playing the latest games. My love for technology and its potential is evident in everything I do, and I am always eager to share my knowledge and experience with others.
